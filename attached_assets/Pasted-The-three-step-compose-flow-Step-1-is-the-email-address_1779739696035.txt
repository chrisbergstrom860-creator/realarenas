The three-step compose flow
Step 1 is the email address table — three pre-populated rows with email, optional first name, and role (Member / Coach / Admin) dropdowns. Adding rows and removing them both update the recipient count live. An "Import CSV" button goes to the dedicated CSV import tab; "Paste list" opens a modal for bulk email entry separated by commas or line breaks. Entering an invalid email format turns the row input red.
Step 2 is the message customiser. The subject line and personal note both update the live email preview on the right in real time as you type — so Rachel can see exactly what Jamie will receive before sending. There's a character counter on the note, a 14-day expiry dropdown with a hint about the auto-reminder, and three toggles: auto-reminder on day 5, notify coach on each acceptance, and an auto-join option (off by default — important for a private club like Hackney RC).
Step 3 shows a pre-send summary (recipients, invite window, auto-reminder, seats remaining after sending) and the yellow send button. Clicking it validates that at least one email contains an "@" and opens the success modal if valid.
The live email preview
The right column shows a full rendered email preview — Chrome bar, From/To/Subject header, club icon and name, Rachel's personal note (pulled live from Step 2's textarea), the feature bullet list, and both CTAs: "Accept invite & create free account" and "Already have an account? Sign in instead". The fallback link (the raw URL) is shown below the buttons for members whose email clients block images. The footer shows the expiry date, unsubscribe link, and privacy policy — the details that make it look trustworthy rather than spam.
Pending invitations table
Below the compose flow is the full pending table showing all 8 outstanding invites. Three rows (David, Natasha, Ben) are highlighted in pale red because they expire in 2 days — the expiry shows "⚠ 2 days" in red text. Yellow "Resend now" buttons on those rows draw the eye. For other pending invites the resend button is grey ghost. Clicking Resend changes the button to "✓ Resent" in green for 3 seconds. Clicking Revoke animates the row out and decrements the sidebar badge count.
CSV import tab
Clicking "Import CSV" in the compose header goes to a dedicated tab with a drag-and-drop zone. Clicking or dropping triggers a simulated upload showing a parsed CSV preview (4 visible rows plus a "+6 more" note) and an import results card (10 valid / 8 with names / 0 duplicates). "Import 10 members to invite form" goes back to the compose tab. Alternative import options (Google Contacts, Strava club members, paste list) are also available.
Invite history tab
Accessible from the sidebar, this shows the full 62-invite history with sent date, responded date, status pills (Accepted / Pending / Expired / Declined), and a mini progress bar showing time-to-accept for each accepted member — a useful signal for Rachel to know whether future invites should go out with more urgency.
