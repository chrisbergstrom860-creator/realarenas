The profile hero
The hero section sits above the tab bar and stays visible on every tab. It uses a dark background with a subtle dot-grid texture and a radial yellow glow — the same treatment as the For Clubs hero. Jamie's avatar has an edit button that appears on hover, a green "online now" dot, and an edit pencil overlay that opens the photo upload modal. To the right of the avatar: name, handle in monospace with join date, sport tags (Running / Cycling / Climbing colour-coded in their own accent colours), level and Pro badges, a bio, and four stats (activities, km, followers, following). On the far right is the club rank badge showing both club rank (#5 Hackney RC) and global rank (#2,841), plus Edit profile and Share profile buttons. Below the main hero row is a connections strip showing five follower avatars with names.
Six tabs across the tab bar — each switches the content area with a smooth scroll to just below the hero:
Overview — the default view. Four stat cards (this week km, this month km, current streak, club rank) each with a colour-coded mini progress bar. Below that in a two-column layout: recent activities (last two logged, each with stats, description, and AI coach analysis), and an 8-week volume bar chart with clickable bars that show the week's total in a toast. The right column has a 28-day activity calendar with colour-coded tiles (rest / easy / medium / hard / race / today), a sport breakdown showing running/cycling/climbing as share bars, a club leaderboard snippet with Jamie highlighted at #5, and active challenges with progress bars.
Activities — full filterable/sortable list of all 312 activities with sport and time filters, export button, and three detailed activity cards showing the same stats+AI+kudos format. Each activity has edit, share, kudos toggle, and comment buttons.
Stats & PRs — personal bests for all three sports. Running section shows 5K (25:48), 10K (53:14), half marathon (1:54:22), and a dashed "no result yet" card for the marathon with the London Marathon target noted. Cycling shows FTP (241W), W/kg, max power, and longest ride. Climbing shows bouldering grade (V5) and lead grade (6b+). The right column has all-time totals (distance, time, elevation, calories, activities) and three goal cards (sub-3:30 marathon, FTP 260W, V6 bouldering) each with a progress bar toward the target.
Achievements — 12 cards in a 3-column grid. Gold badges (first 10km, 7-day streak, century rider, event starter, podium finisher), silver badges (V4 climber, challenge finisher, AI coached ×50), and bronze (club member). Three locked achievements are dimmed and greyscaled — 30-day streak, half marathon PB, and Everest climber — each showing current progress toward the unlock condition.
Clubs — the one club Jamie belongs to (Hackney RC) with sport pill, role badge, join date, and an Open button. Below that a dashed "Find clubs near you" card for discovery.
Following — toggles between Following (89) and Followers (142) modes. A grid of athlete cards each showing name, sport/level, current streak and points, and an "Unfollow" button. Clicking unfollow triggers a toast.
Settings — split into two columns. Left: profile information form (name, handle, bio, city/country, sport chips with active state toggles, level dropdown), then connected accounts with Strava shown as connected with a disconnect button and three unconnected devices with connect buttons. Right: privacy toggles (five settings), notification toggles (six settings), plan card showing Pro with next billing date, and a danger zone with Export data and Delete account buttons.
Three modals — Edit profile (quick form with photo change button), Comments (threaded conversation with typed reply input), photo upload (drag-drop zone), and Add goal (title, sport, date, notes).
