The coach dashboard
your members deserve

Arenas replaces the WhatsApp group, the Google Sheet, and the paper sign-up sheet with one place that actually works.

📊
Training load monitoring
See every member's weekly volume at a glance. Colour-coded load bars flag who's peaking, who's building, and who's at injury risk before they are.
🏆
Private leaderboards
Club-only rankings that update in real time. Weekly, monthly, or all-time. Members compete with each other, not strangers. Huge for retention.
⚡
Club challenges
Set a 100km monthly challenge, a 7-day streak, or a fastest 5K competition. Members see live progress in their feed. You see who's engaged and who isn't.
📅
Event management
Create club events, collect RSVPs, and send coach notes — all inside Arenas. Members RSVP from their feed. You see the full attendance list instantly.
✦
AI coaching for every member
Every activity gets an AI coaching analysis — HR trends, pacing, recovery recommendations. You coach the group. Arenas coaches the individual.
✉️
One-click invite system
Send personalised email invites to members. They click, create their account, and auto-join your club. No approval queue, no manual steps, no friction.

Setup in minutes

Your club is live
before Tuesday's session

No technical setup. No spreadsheet migration. No training your members on new software. Arenas is built to be running in 5 minutes.

1
Create your account
Sign up with your name and email. Your personal Arenas account is created first — you're an athlete too, not just a coach.
2
Set up your club
Name, sport, location, and handle. Takes 60 seconds. Your club page is live immediately with a shareable join link.
3
Invite your members
Paste their emails or upload a CSV. Each member gets a personalised invite with your name on it. One click and they're in.
4
Members log their training
Members log their activities in a few taps and they flow straight into your club dashboard. Automatic Strava and device sync is coming soon.
5
Coach with real data
See training load, flag at-risk members, set challenges, manage events. Everything you used to do manually, done for you.
